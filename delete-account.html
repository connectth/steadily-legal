Delete Your Account & Data

Steadily — Twinhearth Studio

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data from Steadily at any time.

How to request deletion

Send an email to our support address with the subject line "Delete My Account":

twinhearthstudio@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Steadily account so we can locate and delete your data.

What gets deleted

  • Your account credentials (email and password)
  • Your profile name and family membership
  • All messages you have sent
  • All parenting schedule data you have created
  • All mood check-ins logged under your account
  • All child information you have entered
  • All handover records associated with your account

What may be retained

  • Data shared with your co-parent may remain visible to them until they also request deletion
  • Anonymised usage statistics (not linked to your identity)

Timeline

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving your email.
